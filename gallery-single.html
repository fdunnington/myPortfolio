Custom Bootstrap

A project to improve the design, functionality, and usability of a pre-existing custom Bootstrap site

Work With Me

I joined this project after the initial site build, initially working as part of the design team to design and implement new functions. The product team would research ideas for new functions, and pass these on to me to design. As a long term project it was key that the designs could be integrated with the existing site: not standing apart, but slowly bringing in improvements.

An important part of this was being able to work with existing code, implementing changes through Github

For every new feature I design the process starts with an in-depth chat with the product team so we can clarify the purpose of the feature, both as a stand-alone module/page, and within the context of the site, and the business as a whole. From here I move on to rough sketches, then lo-fi mock-ups, hi-fi mock-ups, and eventually to the final design and prototypes.

Once designs are finalised I build the modules so they're ready to pass on to the the dev team to integrate with the site.

Each stage involves feedback sessions & discussions with the product & dev teams to make sure we only move on to the build phase once the design is ready.
