Information Architecture (IA)

Feeling overwhelmed by the quantity of content, or just not sure what should go where?

Sub-optimal Information Architecture can leave a website feeling cluttered and confusing, no matter how great the content. If you're sruggling to organise your content in a way that makes sense for users then addressing the IA is a great way of ensuring that your website's content is laid out for optimal findability and user experience.

Here's how IA can bring order to your digital chaos:

Clear & concise structure
A logical and intuitive website structure makes navigation a breeze, and easy navigation is key to avoiding user frustration. It's also important to great search-engine rankings by ensuring search engines can crawl and index your content correctly, and by promoting user engagement.

Improved findability
Much of UX and content design is about removing friction for website users. If your users effortlessly find the information they need it'll benefit your user engagement and conversion rates.

Enhanced user experience
Logically-structured content feels organised, and fosters trust and brand loyalty.
