The impact of quality copywriting on a business re-brand

This case study explores how well-written and well-structured content played a crucial role in a travel leader's successful rebrand.

The challenge: rejuvenating a dated brand image

Imagine a travel website with faded photos and forgotten destinations. Outdated content, a confusing layout, and a lack of pizazz to their fundamentally exciting product – European lake and mountain holidays. The old content wasn’t resonating with potential customers, and sales were taking a hit in this crowded market.

The fix: content that captivates and converts

We knew a complete content overhaul was needed. Here's how high-quality copywriting turned things around:

  • Engaging descriptions: We swapped out tired info for vivid descriptions that painted a picture of crystal-clear lakes and invigorating mountain hikes.
  • A funnel that funnels: We implemented clear internal linking and a user-friendly layout, making it a breeze for visitors to find the info they wanted.
  • Accessibility for all: Ensuring the website met WCAG3 accessibility standards was crucial.
  • SEO savvy: Keyword optimisation put the focus firmly on walking, attracting a broader audience searching for active yet accessible mountain adventures.
  • Speaking the right language: We developed a consistent tone, style, and terminology guide that reflected the brand's re-invention while keeping its core identity intact.

The outcome: a revitalised brand

The end result is a website that speaks volumes – not just to their existing loyal customers, but to a whole new generation of summer explorers.

By crafting content that captivates and informs, you can breathe new life into your brand, draw in new customers, and ultimately drive sales success.
