The impact of quality copywriting on a business re-brand

This case study explores how well-written and well-structured content played a starring role in a travel leader's successful re-brand.

The challenge: Rejuvenating a dated brand image

Imagine a travel website filled with faded photos and forgotten destinations. Inaccurate content, a confusing layout, and a lack of focus on their exciting summer product – European lake and mountain holidays. The outdated content wasn't resonating with potential customers, leading to missed opportunities.

The fix: Content that captivates and converts

We knew a complete content refresh was needed. Here's how high-quality copywriting addressed some of the challenges:

  • Engaging descriptions: We replaced tired information with vivid descriptions tailored to each destination, painting a picture of crystal-clear lakes and invigorating mountain hikes.
  • An engaging funnel: We implemented clear internal linking and a user-friendly layout, making it a breeze for visitors to find the information they wanted.
  • Accessibility for all: Ensuring the website met WCAG3 accessibility standards was crucial.
  • SEO savvy: Keyword optimisation put the focus firmly on walking, attracting a broader audience searching for active but accessible mountain adventures.
  • Speaking the right language: We developed a consistent tone, style, and terminology guide that reflected the brand's re-invention while retaining its core identity.

The outcome: A brand revitalised

The end result is a website that speaks volumes – not just to their existing loyal customer base, but to a whole new generation of summer explorers.

This case study showcases how quality copywriting can be a game-changer in a business re-brand. By creating content that captivates and informs, you can breathe new life into your brand, attract new customers, and ultimately drive sales success.
