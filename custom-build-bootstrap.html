Custom Bootstrap

A project to improve the design, functionality, and feature scope of an existing custom-built Bootstrap gaming platform

Work With Me

The Project

I joined this project after the initial site build as part of the design team. Our focus was the design and implementation of new features for the site.

The product team would research ideas for new functions, passing them on to the design team to create and implement. A key focus was ensuring a modular approach to the building of the features, allowing for ease of integration in the existing site.

The Process

Research: For every new feature the process starts with an in-depth chat with the product team so we can clarify the purpose of the feature as a stand-alone module, within the context of the site, and within the business as a whole.

Design: Once the research was complete we would move on to rough sketches, then lo-fi mock-ups, hi-fi mock-ups, and eventually to the final design and prototypes. The design process involves feedback sessions & discussions with the product & dev teams to make sure we only move on to the build phase once the design is ready.

Build: Once designs are finalised we converted the designs to stand-alone Bootstrap modules that were ready to pass on to the the dev team for integration with the site.
