Modernising a WooCommerce Site

A project to re-design a ten-year-old publishing website built with WooCommerce/Wordpress.

Work With Me

The team at Piccadilly were looking to upgrade and modernise a tired site.

Their existing site was built in Wordpress using WooCommerce for its ecommerce platform The goals were to modernise the site, offering users a fresh new design that was aimed at attracting a younger market, but the company were keen to keep the site on the existing platform as the staff were familiar with how Wordpress/WooCommerce work, and the company wanted updates/changes to be possible for the staff to do themselves.

I began the project putting together a site map with the company director so we could clarify what of the existing site needed keeping, and where would be best to include it in the new site.

With the site architecture mapped out I put together some Figma mock-ups with a few design ideas, focusing on colours, fonts, and the feel of the site.

Once we'd settled on a design I worked on prototypes for each page in our site map so the client was able to see how the site would look and function on a variety of screen sizes. This enabled them to see where any changes were needed before we started building.

From a pre-determined design I was able to quickly build and deploy the site using Elementor, giving the client a fresh and better-functioning site, but with a familiar backend that ensured the client and their team were able to continue using the site without any re-training.
